Get MetaTrader 5 from your broker

MT5 is free from any regulated broker (IC Markets, Pepperstone, etc.). Open a demo account first to practice.

  1. Choose a regulated broker in your country.
  2. Download MT5 and log in with broker credentials.
  3. Open a chart — e.g. XAUUSD or EURUSD on H1.

Install AuraFX on MT5

Clients (recommended): buy or rent on MQL5 Market → Tools → Market → search AuraFX → Install.

Seller (you): publish compiled products — see Publish on MQL5.

  1. Enable Algo Trading if using an Expert Advisor.
  2. Drag indicator from Navigator → Indicators onto chart.
  3. Load preset from MQL5/Presets/ if provided.

How you actually trade (important)

AuraFX does not execute trades for you and is not a broker.

  1. Use AuraFX labels, checklist, and calendar as education — not automatic instructions.
  2. In MT5, you right-click the chart → Trading → New Order (or use one-click trading).
  3. Set your own lot size, stop loss, and take profit with your broker.
  4. EAs (if used) run on your VPS/PC — test on demo first in Strategy Tester.

Trading leveraged products is high risk. You may lose all deposited capital. Past analysis is not indicative of future results.
