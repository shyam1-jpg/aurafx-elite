Install AuraFX on MT5

Clients install from MQL5 Market. You publish products from your AuraFX-Elite project folder.

Clients — after buying on MQL5

  1. Open MetaTrader 5 from your broker (demo account recommended first).
  2. Tools → Market (Ctrl+M) → Purchases → Install.
  3. Navigator → Indicators or Expert Advisors → drag onto chart.
  4. Enable Algo Trading for EAs.
  5. You place trades in MT5 — AuraFX shows analysis labels only.
