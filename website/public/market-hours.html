Key overlaps (highest liquidity)

London / New York

~13:00–17:00 UTC. Peak liquidity for EURUSD, GBPUSD, XAUUSD. Spreads often tightest.

Tokyo / London

~08:00–09:00 UTC. Handoff period; watch USDJPY and cross pairs.

Off-hours

~22:00–00:00 UTC gap. Thinner books; wider spreads possible on some brokers.

Gold (XAUUSD) notes

  • Gold trades nearly 24h on most brokers; sharp moves often around US data (13:30 UTC) and FOMC.
  • Correlates inversely with USD strength and real yields — educational context only in AuraFX panels.
  • Use economic calendar before trading around CPI, NFP, FOMC.
