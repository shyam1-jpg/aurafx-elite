How consent works

When you first visit, a banner lets you choose Accept all or Essential only. Your choice is stored in local storage as aurafx_cookie_consent. Non-essential scripts (Google Fonts) load only if you accept all.

Third parties

  • PayPal — may set cookies when you pay (only when checkout is enabled).
  • Google Fonts — loaded only after “Accept all”.
  • Market data APIs — Yahoo Finance / CoinGecko called from our server; your browser does not send personal data to them beyond normal HTTP requests initiated by our pages.

Control

Clear site data in your browser to reset consent. Blocking essential storage may break registration and the consent banner memory.

To change preference: clear aurafx_cookie_consent in browser storage and reload the page.
