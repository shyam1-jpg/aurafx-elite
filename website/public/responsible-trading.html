Responsible Trading & Gambling-Style Harm Policy

Last updated: June 2026

1. Purpose

AuraFX provides trading technology and education, not licensed gambling services. However, frequent trading, leverage, and “signal chasing” can cause harm similar to problem gambling. We promote responsible behaviour.

2. This is not gambling

Our products are not bets on random events like sports or slots. Markets have risk but are not games of chance. Still, compulsive trading is a real health and financial risk.

3. Warning signs

  • Trading with borrowed money or money needed for living costs
  • Chasing losses or increasing size after losses
  • Neglecting sleep, work, or family due to charts
  • Hiding losses from others
  • Feeling unable to stop despite repeated losses

4. Our commitments

  • Clear risk warnings on registration and Risk warning page
  • No promises of guaranteed returns in our marketing
  • Encourage demo accounts and risk limits before live trading
  • We may suspend accounts that appear fraudulent or abusive

5. Your responsibilities

  • Use stop-losses and position sizing; never risk more than you can lose
  • Take breaks; do not trade under alcohol, drugs, or extreme stress
  • Use broker tools: negative balance protection (where offered), margin alerts

6. Help & self-exclusion

If trading feels out of control, seek help:

  • UK: BeGambleAware, GamCare (also supports trading addiction)
  • International: contact local mental health or addiction services
  • Ask your broker to restrict or close your trading account

7. Minors

Under-18s must not register. We will remove accounts if we identify a minor.

8. Marketing

We avoid targeting vulnerable groups or implying trading is “easy money” or entertainment gambling.
