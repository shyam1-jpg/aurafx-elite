Risk Warning

Last updated: June 2026

Capital at risk

Contracts for difference (CFDs), spread bets, forex, metals, indices, and other leveraged instruments are complex and carry a high level of risk. Price movements can be rapid and unpredictable. Losses can exceed your deposit if your broker permits negative balance or high leverage.

Retail CFD disclosure (UK FCA style)

Trading leveraged products involves significant risk of loss. Most retail client accounts lose money when trading CFDs and similar products. AuraFX Elite does not remove or reduce these risks — our tools are for analysis and education only.

We do not refer you to a specific CFD provider and do not display broker loss statistics on this site. Before trading with any broker, read their own risk disclosure, including the percentage of retail accounts that lose money with that provider.

Not suitable for everyone

Do not trade with money you cannot afford to lose (rent, bills, essential savings). Seek independent financial advice if you are unsure whether trading is appropriate for you.
