================================================================================
  AuraFX Elite — HOW CUSTOMERS REGISTER, PAY, LEARN & USE MT5
================================================================================

This is the full journey from sign-up to trading on MetaTrader 5.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
CHANNEL A — Website subscription (aurafxelite.com)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

STEP 1 — DISCOVER
  Customer visits https://aurafxelite.com
  Reads plans: Free dashboard, MT5 bundle ($99), monthly rent ($15), MT4 waitlist

STEP 2 — REGISTER (3 steps on register.html)
  1. Name, email, phone, country, broker, experience
  2. Choose plan + accept legal policies (terms, privacy, risk warning)
  3. Pay with PayPal (or Free plan → skip payment)

STEP 3 — PAY (PayPal — secure, encrypted by PayPal)
  • Card/bank details NEVER touch your AuraFX server
  • PayPal handles payment with bank-level encryption (TLS + PCI)
  • Your server VERIFIES the payment with PayPal before marking "paid"
  • Customer sees success → redirected to dashboard.html

STEP 4 — EMAIL VERIFICATION
  • Verification link sent to their email (when RESEND_API_KEY is set)
  • Confirms they own the email address

STEP 5 — WEB DASHBOARD (immediate — no MT5 required)
  dashboard.html gives:
  • Live news & economic calendar
  • Market mood / risk warnings
  • Journal, screenshots, learning tools
  • Links to MT5 install guide

STEP 6 — GET MT5 SOFTWARE (two ways)

  Option A — MQL5 Market (recommended for paying customers)
    1. Install MetaTrader 5 from broker (free)
    2. Tools → Market → search "AuraFX"
    3. Buy/rent product → Install (MetaQuotes handles licence)
    4. Navigator → Indicators → drag AuraFX onto chart

  Option B — Website bundle ($99 plan)
    After payment you (owner) can:
    • Send MQL5 Market voucher / invite, OR
    • Email install link to mt5-install.html on your site
    Customer follows mt5-install.html steps

STEP 7 — LEARN & USE ON CHART
  • Open DEMO account first (not real money)
  • Chart: XAUUSD or EURUSD, H1 recommended
  • Attach AuraFX_Elite_Signals indicator
  • Panel shows BUY / SELL / COVER signals + risk stats
  • Optional: AuraFX_Elite_EA in Strategy Tester (backtest)
  • Risk Guardian warns before high-impact news

STEP 8 — "EARNING" (realistic expectations)
  • Software shows signals and risk tools — NOT guaranteed profit
  • Customer trades their own broker account
  • They control lot size, stop loss, and when to trade
  • AuraFX does not hold customer funds (broker does)
  • Past performance ≠ future results (stated in legal pages)

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
CHANNEL B — MQL5 Market only (no website registration)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

  Customer finds AuraFX on mql5.com → pays MetaQuotes/PayPal there
  → downloads into MT5 automatically
  → website dashboard is optional (free tier still available)

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
WHAT YOU (OWNER) SEE
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

  owner-leads.html — paid registrations (owner key required)
  PayPal business account — actual money received
  MQL5 seller dashboard — marketplace sales

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
WHY PAYPAL TEST FAILED FOR YOU
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

  • No website/.env file with PAYPAL_CLIENT_ID + PAYPAL_CLIENT_SECRET
